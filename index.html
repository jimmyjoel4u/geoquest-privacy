GeoQuest Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026 · App: GeoQuest — World Geography Trivia (com.geoquest.app)

GeoQuest is a geography trivia game designed for players of all ages, including children. We collect as little information as possible, and this policy explains exactly what the app does with data, in plain language.

1. Information We Collect

  • An anonymous player ID. When you play, Firebase (a Google service) creates a random identifier for your device. It is not your name, email, or phone number, and we cannot identify you from it.
  • Your chosen explorer nickname and cartoon avatar. You pick these in the app. We recommend children do not use their real full name — the app never requires it.
  • Gameplay data. Scores, XP, levels, streaks, achievements, and duel results. This is stored on your device and synced to our online database (Google Firestore) under your anonymous ID so leaderboards and cloud save work.
  • Google account info — only for players 13 and older. Players in the 13+ age group may optionally connect a Google account to save progress across devices. This shares the account's name and email address with the app. This option is never shown to players in the under-13 age groups.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

No location data. No contacts. No photos, camera, or microphone. No phone number. No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. No behavioral advertising profiles.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

GeoQuest asks for an age group when you first play. For players under 13:

  • We do not collect personal information (no Google sign-in is offered).
  • Ads are served in Google AdMob's child-directed mode: non-personalized, rated G, with no behavioral targeting and no use of the advertising ID for profiling.
  • Gameplay data is tied only to the anonymous ID.

Parents: to review or delete your child's data, contact us at the email below and we will remove the cloud record for the anonymous ID.

4. Advertising

The app shows ads via Google AdMob, configured for child-directed treatment with a maximum content rating of G. Rewarded ads (e.g., "watch an ad for a heart") are always optional and clearly labeled. See Google's Privacy Policy for how Google processes ad requests.

5. Purchases

Optional in-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment details. Purchases can be managed or cancelled in the Google Play app.

6. Where Data Lives & Security

  • On your device: settings, hearts, and progress are stored locally. The in-app "Reset All Data" button erases them.
  • In the cloud: gameplay stats sync to Google Firestore over encrypted (HTTPS) connections, protected by security rules that only allow each player to write their own data.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Local data is deleted when you use "Reset All Data" or uninstall the app. Cloud data is kept while your account is active; email us to request deletion and we will remove it within 30 days.

8. Your Rights (GDPR & similar laws)

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the data linked to your player ID at any time using the contact below. Because most data is anonymous, please contact us from within the affected device where possible so we can locate the right record.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (authentication & database) — Firebase privacy
  • Google AdMob (ads, child-directed mode) — AdMob & families
  • Google Play Billing (purchases)

10. Changes to This Policy

If we change how the app handles data, we will update this page and the effective date above before the change ships.
